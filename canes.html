2. RAISING CANE'S

"Raising Cane's is great. I understand the argument that Canes is carried by the sauce and in my opinion, I agree with it. But the sauce is the whole reason why it's so good. It's more seasoned than the chicken because the whole point is to dip the chicken in it. Again like Wingstop, the fries are whatever but the bread is great, even the coleslaw too. Honestly it's one of those places you crave specifically for the sauce, not just the chicken. Super simple menu but it always hits when you want something reliable."
