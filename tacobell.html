4. TACO BELL

Taco Bell is great, decent variety and lots of value meals. Obviously can’t beat a street taco stand but their hard-shell tacos are great. Bringing back the quesarito was a gamechanger in this list for me as I used to get it a lot back in the day. Nacho fries are too good, highly recommend. Overall Taco Bell is one of those places that can go absolutely nuclear late at night. Honestly it’s a classic late-night clutch spot when nothing else is open. Sometimes you just randomly crave it and nothing else hits the same.
