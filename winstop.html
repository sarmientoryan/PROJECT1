1. WINGSTOP

I absolutely love, adore and admire Wingstop. Garlic Parmesan and Hot Honey are too good. Usually get bone-in wings but sometimes get boneless or tenders here and there. The fries are whatever but the ranch is top 5 creations out there. Can be pretty expensive but it hits when it needs to. Honestly one of my go–to comfort meals after a long day, especially late at night. Buffalo Wild Wings is not it, just get Wingstop.

MY FAVORITE FLAVORS

GARLIC PARMESEAN

Savory garlic, buttery parmesan. This dry rub is simple, potent, and delicious.

HOT HONEY

Sweet honey in a fiery dry rub.

LEMON PEPPER

Zesty lemon. Cracked black pepper. This dry rub is the ultimate gateway flavor.
