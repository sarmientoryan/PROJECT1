3. MCDONALDS

Mcdonalds is a go to. Fries hit when they need to but you can get unlucky and get some bland and soggy fries. Mcdonalds App is goated easy to get discounts to get a good amount of food. Me liking the fillet–o–fish might be a hot take but i'll stand on that hill. Lots of variety in the menu, breakfast is great, food is even better late at night, overall decent number 3.

MY FAVORITE ITEMS

Chicken Nuggets

All white meat Chicken Nuggets with no artificial preservatives!

French Fries

McDonald's World Famous Fries are made with premium potatoes such as the Russet Burbank and the Shepody.

Fillet-O-Fish sandwich

A delicious fish sandwich made with wild-caught Alaskan Pollock, cheese & tartar sauce!
