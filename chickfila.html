5. Chick–fil–A

To me Chick–fil–A is number 5 mainly because I only get it when I really crave it. They have the best fries in these 5 fast food spots with McDonald's or Taco Bell nacho fries being number 2. It's on the pricier side and if I'm paying 15 dollars for chicken and fries I might as well get Wingstop. Still though, the quality is always consistent and the chicken sandwich definitely delivers. Just not something I go out of my way for often.
